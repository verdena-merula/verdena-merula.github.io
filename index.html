World of Eltharia

I'm Verdena Merula — a part-time author with a full-time love for world-building, magic systems, and the stories that live quietly between the lines.

Eltharia is the world I keep returning to: a land of ancient magic, fractured kingdoms, and characters who refuse to stay still. Every story I write here is a new door into a world I've been building for years.

Pull up a chair. The fire's warm, the tea is ready, and the stories are just beginning.
